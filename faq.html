Frequently Asked Questions

How does the image compressor work?
Our tool reduces the file size of your images using smart compression algorithms while maintaining quality.
Is there a limit to the number of images I can compress?
No, you can compress as many images as you like, completely free of charge.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP formats.
Will the quality of my images be affected?
Our compressor is designed to preserve the visual quality of your images while significantly reducing their size.
Do I need to install any software?
No installation is needed. Our image compressor runs entirely in your browser.
Is my image data safe?
Yes, we do not store your images. All compression is done securely and your files are deleted after processing.
Can I compress images on my mobile device?
Absolutely! Our tool is fully responsive and works smoothly on smartphones and tablets.
Do you offer bulk image compression?
Yes, you can upload and compress multiple images at once for increased efficiency.
